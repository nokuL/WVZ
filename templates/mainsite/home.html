About us


Womenâ€™s voice Zimbabwe is a registered media organization that seeks to amplify the voice for the women and girls in Zimbabwe. The organisation was established in 2017 after realising that most women are consumers of content and not creators of content, hence most issues affecting women where not being reported or being under reported in the media. Our goal is to inform, empower and advocate for women and girls. We believe in celebrating women and girls successes, defending their rights, highlighting their plight, mentoring, sharing their experiences and ambitions.
